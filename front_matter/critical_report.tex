\chapter*{Critical Report.}

This edition bases upon a bases upon a copy in the Dommusikarchiv Eisenstadt. The digital version of the manuscript is available at \url{http://dommusikarchiv.martinus.at/site/werkverzeichnis/gallery/293.html} (siglum G 112).

In general, this edition closely follows the manuscript. Any changes that were introduced by the editor are indicated by italic type (lyrics, dynamics and directions), parentheses (expressive marks) or dashes (slurs and ties). Accidentals are used according to modern conventions. Asterisks denote changes that are clarified in the detailed remarks below.\footnote{Abbreviations: A, alto; B, basso; b, basses; clno, clarion; Ms, manuscript; org, organ; r, rest; S, soprano; T, tenor; timp, timpani; vl, violin; vla, viola.}
